MAKE YOURSELF AT HOME

Freshly opened in 2020, “Uvacome hotel” provides a home away from home. The name “Uvacome hotel” comes from ancient Italia mythology and is one of the Nereid nymphs, protector of the Ha Noi. The Nereides were protectors of sailors and fishermen, coming to the aid of those in distress. At Uvacome, we focus on creating a home-like feeling for our guests while paying special attention to details like our beds, pillows and linens for the best possible quality of sleep. Our rooms are decorated in pastels and earthly tones that exude harmony and serenity, with simple yet chic furniture of natural wood.


Eco-friendly and natural materials such as oak, linen and cotton are used, along with some selective handcrafted items by Italia designers. Among the sleek, modern architecture you will also find some elements of traditional island architecture, like our ceilings with beams and reeds. Natural sunlight flows though the space while lighting is set up attentively to brighten each room creating an atmosphere that encourages you to spread out and find a quiet corner to relax. No two rooms are decorated exactly alike, further reinforcing the home-style feel.
