Fitness Centre
Available when registering packages:
Price from: $100
or Free to receive membership card when book a room
Open hours: 24 hours ( Mon - Sat )

At one of the world's most spectacular Fitness Centre locations, guests can utilise the latest in ultra-modern equipment while looking over sweeping views of Dong Anh Lake. Take advantage of an extensive range of cardio, strength and weight training equipment by Life Fitness. Post-workout, take time to unwind in five-star locker room facilities including a hot whirlpool, cold plunge pool, sauna and steam rooms.

Facilities and Equipment

This luxurious gym features a semi-private streching area, television and iPod docking stations on all cardiovascular equipment and VIVO digital workout tracking technology. Personal trainers are on hand to offer one-on-one coaching sessions and assist members with a customized fitness program.
